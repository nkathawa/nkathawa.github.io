About Me

My name is Navin Kathawa, and I am a senior studying computer science at the University of Michigan. I decided to major in computer science during my freshman year of college, when I took the introductory programming course for engineers, and found it to be quite fun and interesting.

I have since gone on to take a variety of programming and computer science courses, and I have held two software engineering internships - one at Servant Systems and the other at Billhighway. In addition to my coursework and work experience, I am on the executive board for Code-M, a computer science club on campus which, among other things, organizes programming workshops for those who are interested in learning more about programming and computer science.

On a more personal level, I am an avid lover of both watching and playing hockey. I am a diehard fan of the Detroit Red Wings, but I'll watch no matter who is playing. Additionally, I a member of the club roller hockey team at U of M. I also enjoy hanging out with friends, reading, lifting weights, listening to rap and hip hop, and eating good food.

This personal website was created from scratch using HTML, CSS, JS, JQuery, and Bootstrap. I hope you enjoy it and find something interesting!
