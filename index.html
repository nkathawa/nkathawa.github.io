About Me

My name is Navin Kathawa, and I'm a software engineer. I was previously with Nutshell doing backend development in PHP. Before that, I was employed at Domino's in the role of Site Reliability & Dev Ops Engineer, where I was responsible for Kubernetes development, maintaining and improving the infrastructure, automating deployments and other tasks, and troubleshooting.

Prior to working as a Site Reliability & Dev Ops Engineer, I worked through a Technology Rotation Program at Domino's. I rotated through different pieces of the Domino's technology stack in order to gain a broader understanding of the company as a whole and to improve my software development skills. The areas through which I rotated were web as a backend developer, store technology as a backend developer, and site reliability.

Previously, I was a student in the College of Engineering at the University of Michigan, where I obtained a bachelor's degree in computer science. I was also on the executive board for Code M, a computer science organization on campus.

On a more personal level, I am an avid lover of both watching and playing hockey. I am a diehard fan of the Detroit Red Wings, but I'll watch no matter who is playing. I also enjoy hanging out with friends, reading, lifting weights, listening to rap and hip hop, and eating good food.

This personal website was created from scratch using HTML, CSS, JS, JQuery, and Bootstrap. I hope you enjoy it and find something interesting!
