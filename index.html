Who Am I?

My name is Navin Kathawa, and I am a junior studying computer science at the University of Michigan. I decided to major in computer science during my freshman year of college, when I took the mandatory programming course for engineers, and found that it was something that I very much enjoyed.

I have since gone on to take a variety of programming and computer science courses, and I held a software engineering internship last summer at Servant Systems, Inc. In addition to my coursework, I am involved in Code-M, a computer science club on campus, in which I am responsible for organizing programming workshops for those who are interested in learning more about programming and computer science. I am also a member of the club roller hockey team here at U of M.

On a more personal level, I am an avid lover of both watching and playing hockey. I am a diehard Wings and Capitals fan, but I am a lover of the sport, so I'll watch and enjoy no matter who is playing. I also enjoy hanging out with friends, reading, playing video games (XBOX especially), lifting weights, and eating good food!
